Duques Hall Study Room Policies

A GWorld card enabled for campus access is required for entrance to Duques Hall.
Access to the reservation software is only available using an @gwu.edu account.
Rooms are available for reservation on a first come, first served basis.
Reservations may be placed up to two weeks in advance.
A student may place up to 2 hours of reservations each day.
When not reserved, an open study room may be used by one or more people for study. If someone arrives with a reservation, the room must be vacated for their use immediately.
Windows may not be covered for any reason. Anyone found inside a study room with the windows covered may be banned from future room reservations.
